Ingredient Reminders

⚠️Never use Retinol and Lactic Acid back-to-back — spaced Tue/Thu intentionally.
⚠️TXA is in both the brightening pad AND the Anua 10+TXA serum — never use both same day.
⚠️Always follow Retinol with Anua Ceramide to protect your skin barrier.
⚠️SPF every morning — especially after dermaplaning, retinol, or any acid night.
⚠️No brightening actives on dermaplane nights — skin is already freshly exfoliated.
